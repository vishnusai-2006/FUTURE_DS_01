# Business Sales Performance Analysis

## Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Google Colab

## Key Insights
- Sales show an increasing trend over time
- Technology category generates highest revenue
- West region performs best
- Top 10 products contribute significantly to total revenue

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts in high-performing regions
- Improve performance in South region
- Prioritize Technology category inventory
- Maintain stock of top-selling products
