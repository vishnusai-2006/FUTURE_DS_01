# Task 1: Business Sales Performance Analysis

## Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

## Key Insights
- Sales show an overall increasing trend over time.
- Technology category generates the highest revenue.
- West region performs the strongest.
- A small group of top products contributes significantly to total revenue.

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
- Improve strategy for underperforming regions.
- Prioritize high-revenue product inventory.
- Plan campaigns based on seasonal trends.

# Task 2: Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Objective
Identify churn patterns, retention drivers, and customer lifetime trends for a telecom subscription business.

## Key Findings
- Overall churn rate: ~26.6%.
- Month-to-month contracts show highest churn.
- Customers with low tenure churn more frequently.
- Higher monthly charges increase churn probability.
- Fiber optic service shows higher churn rate.

## Recommendations
- Encourage long-term contract adoption.
- Focus retention strategies on new customers.
- Review pricing structure for high monthly charge plans.
- Improve fiber optic service quality and customer experience.

# Task 3: Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Objective
Analyze marketing funnel performance to identify conversion rates, drop-offs, and channel effectiveness.

## Funnel Overview
- Total Leads: 8000
- Closed Deals: 842
- Overall Conversion Rate: ~10.5%

## Channel Performance Insights
- Paid Search and Organic Search perform best.
- Email and Display channels show lower conversion.
- Significant drop-off between qualified leads and closed deals.

## Recommendations
- Increase investment in high-performing channels.
- Improve email marketing personalization.
- Optimize sales follow-up process.
- Reduce funnel drop-offs through targeted strategies.
